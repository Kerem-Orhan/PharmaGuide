Welcome to **Pharmaceuticals** — your trusted source for clear, accurate, and up-to-date pharmaceutical information.

## Our Mission
To provide reliable drug information, medication guides, and health resources in a simple and easy-to-understand format.

## What We Cover
- Prescription and over-the-counter medications
- Drug interactions and side effects
- Dosage guidelines
- Latest pharmaceutical news and research

*Always consult a licensed healthcare professional before making any medical decisions.*
