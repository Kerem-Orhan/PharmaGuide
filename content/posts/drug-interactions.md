Drug interactions occur when one medication affects how another works in your body. Some interactions can be dangerous, while others may just reduce effectiveness.

## Common Interactions to Avoid

### Blood Thinners + Aspirin
Taking aspirin alongside blood thinners like warfarin can significantly increase bleeding risk.

### Antidepressants + Certain Pain Killers
Some antidepressants combined with tramadol can cause serotonin syndrome, a potentially serious condition.

### Antibiotics + Dairy
Certain antibiotics like tetracycline are absorbed poorly when taken with dairy products.

## Tips to Stay Safe
- Always tell your doctor **every** medication you are taking
- Use a single pharmacy so they can track your medications
- Never stop or start a medication without consulting your doctor first
