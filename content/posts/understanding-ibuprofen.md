Ibuprofen is a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) commonly used to relieve pain, reduce fever, and treat inflammation.

## Common Uses
- Headaches and migraines
- Muscle aches and joint pain
- Fever reduction
- Menstrual cramps

## Dosage Guidelines
| Age | Dose | Frequency |
|-----|------|-----------|
| Adults | 200–400mg | Every 4–6 hours |
| Children (6–12) | 100–200mg | Every 6–8 hours |

## Side Effects to Watch For
- Stomach upset or nausea
- Dizziness
- Increased blood pressure with long-term use

*Do not exceed 1200mg per day without medical supervision.*
