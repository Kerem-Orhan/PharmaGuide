Antibiotics are powerful medicines used to treat bacterial infections. They work by either killing bacteria or stopping them from reproducing, allowing your immune system to clear the infection.

## Common Types of Antibiotics
- **Penicillin** — One of the oldest and most widely used antibiotics
- **Amoxicillin** — Used for ear, throat, and chest infections
- **Azithromycin** — Often prescribed for respiratory infections

## Important Notes
- Antibiotics **do not work** against viral infections like the flu or common cold
- Always complete the full course even if you feel better
- Overuse of antibiotics leads to antibiotic resistance

*Consult your doctor before taking any antibiotic.*
